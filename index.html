About

Derek Cantrell

Derek Cantrell is a high school principal at Alleghany High School in Virginia and the 2026 Virginia Association of Secondary School Principals (VASSP) Outstanding High School Principal of the Year.

  • High school principal at Alleghany High School
  • Author of Better Together
  • Speaker and consultant
  • 2026 VASSP Outstanding High School Principal of the Year

He is a recognized leader in school culture, instructional innovation, and leading through change. With nine years of experience as a principal, he has played a key role in the successful consolidation of two rival high schools into one unified school community, building a strong and positive culture centered on connection, collaboration, and shared success.

Under his leadership, Alleghany High School has implemented impactful initiatives focused on student engagement, staff recognition, and academic improvement. His work emphasizes relationships, clear systems, and intentional culture-building to drive both academic and organizational success.

Derek is a frequent presenter at state and national conferences, including the Virginia Association of Secondary School Principals (VASSP) and the Making Schools Work Conference in New Orleans. He has also been featured on education leadership podcasts, including the Proud Principal Podcast, Principal Matters, and Better Leaders Better Schools.

In addition to his leadership work, Derek is an author and speaker focused on helping schools navigate change while maintaining a strong, people-centered culture. His message centers on the idea that schools are strongest when they operate with a shared vision and a commitment to being Better Together.

Better Together Philosophy

People. Systems. Consistency.

Culture is not one more thing on a leader's plate. It is the plate. Better Together Consulting helps teams turn shared values into repeatable leadership habits that make people feel seen, supported, and ready to grow.

People

Build trust through relationships, visibility, listening, and moments that help staff and students feel noticed and valued.

Systems

Create clear routines for communication, meetings, recognition, data conversations, and instructional follow-through.

Consistency

Reinforce what matters daily so culture is taught, modeled, celebrated, and sustained beyond a single event.

The Book

Better Together: A Leadership Playbook for Culture, Change, and Unity

Derek's book tells the story behind the work: two proud rival high schools, decades of tradition, and the leadership challenge of helping students, staff, families, and a community build something new together.

The book is both story and playbook. It shares practical lessons on communication, trust, student voice, recognition, data leadership, AI in schools, daily positivity, and the first 90 days of leading change. Its central message is clear: culture does not happen by accident. It is built through daily choices, relationships, and consistent systems.

"If we didn't get the culture right, nothing else would work."

"Culture had to be intentionally built, day by day."
